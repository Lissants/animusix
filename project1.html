What is ANIMAX MUSIX?

ANIMAX MUSIX is an annual live concert event broadcasted only in Animax, Japan; where the first concert was held in 2009.

Where is it hosted?

ANIMAX MUSIX live concerts are mainly hosted in Yokohama Arena and Osaka Castle Hall, Japan.

However, ANIMAX MUSIX is also hosted at other areas in Japan such as Kobe and Osaka and Taiwan.

What is My Project About?

In this project, I want to create a place where the ANIMAX MUSIX enthusiasts interact with each other, by providing:

  1. Comprehensive Setlist Archive

    2. By creating a comprehensive archive of all ANIMAX MUSIX concerts, the fans can browse the archive by concert year, artist name, or song title to explore past performances.


  2. Song Name & Artist Database

    3. The site will have a dedicated database of artists who have performed at ANIMAX MUSIX and the songs they have covered. The profile of each artists will be included with their biography and links to their official websites or social media pages.


  3. Concert Review & Fan Experience

    4. This feature will allow fans to submit reviews and share their experience attending ANIMAX MUSIX live concerts or watching the broadcast. Users can rate performances, share photos and videos, and discuss their favorite moments from the concerts.
